Feral Robotic Birds

Birdsong adapts and responds to sonic changes in the environment. Using nightingale songs as a start-point, Feral Robotic Birds speculate the future of birdsong by listening to their environments, and slowly incorporating the sonic history of those environments into their ever-changing songs.
