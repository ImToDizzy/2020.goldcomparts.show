A Magical AI Angle

A Magical AI Angle

A prototype for the sonic part of an interactive installation. The piece takes viewer to an insecure AI oracle that reads aloud predictions about its own future. The name comes from one of the phrases in AI predictions. Built with MaxMSP, Arduino and a text generation RNN in Tensorflow.
