Hazel Ryan

Middle Ground

Middle Ground

This is an experimentation with semi-structured gamelike formats and mining the uncanny through play. It’s a futile chase around memories and terrors, or otherwise a meditation on change, or otherwise a collection of vignettes. The ‘middle ground’ here is that between remembering and letting go, fearing and desiring, seeking and escaping.
