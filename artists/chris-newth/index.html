Computational Spinning Wheel

This installation 'weaves' and controls patterns dependent on the position / speed of a spinning wheel. Designed to be used as a VJ tool / instrument or as a playful installation, I was inspired by a traditional spinning wheel and other spinning machines, such as a lathe and a potter's wheel.
