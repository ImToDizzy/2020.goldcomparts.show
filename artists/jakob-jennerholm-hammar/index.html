Weaving Drafts at the Edge of the Abyss

Weaving Drafts at the Edge of the Abyss

A series of experimental explorations into digital weaving drafts/notation. The project is founded on a series of vague questions about time, nature, relationality and pattern-making which are engaged with through the creation of generative and interactive digital and physical weaving drafts.
